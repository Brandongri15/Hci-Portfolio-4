AR & VR

Introduction

AR & VR is a technology of the future and it enables us to be immersed into a world unlike our own. There is a lot room and ideas for various industries, the sad truth is that the technology is not there or too expensive for an industrial use. With time we will be seeing new leaps and innovations coming out that we only can imagine now.

I would like to add on the imagination of ideas with three of my very own concepts.


Assignment 1 - Concept Creation

Concept one
is for Injured sports player, specifically for this example an injured soccer player. Often Player sit months in rehabilitation needing to train on their own or going through therapy. In this concept Players will be immersed into the world of the football field while nursing their mental and physical health back. User would be put on a larger treadmill where they can freely run and to have the experience of the ball you would have a ball attached to one end that can freely rotate with the player and extends the ball out to them when they in the VR the ball approaches them. The spring inside the arm could also adjust the strength to protect the Player from further injuries.




Concept two
would be an AR application that would represent the world without Humans. So you can go to various Area and see an interpretation of various scientist of how the world would grow without Humans. By showing this we can incentivise people to understand that we can live here but we should also respect what we have been given. The human ego is great for us to further evolve.




The Third Concept
and final is a simulation of physics on real world objects. So say you looking at a bridge and you are wondering how did they manage to keep that thing in the air or how does that support so much traffic. Assignment 2 - Research
