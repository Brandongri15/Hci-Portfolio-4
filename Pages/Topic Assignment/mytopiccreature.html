Assignment 1 - Thought Experiment | Printer

1. Ink Cartridges
The Printer requires Ink to be able to function. Without ink he has no way of actually printing anything that we can see. This need can be compared to the human spine, as we humans require our spine to be able to send signal to our muscles for them to react to what we want to tell them. It like the messenger. For the printer the ink cartridges carry the same responsibility.

2.Paper Tray
A printer needs to have a full paper tray to be able to print, this can be compared to food for Humans. The paper is very simply put its resource panel without it cannot function. We humans cannot function without nutrients, and that is what paper is for a printer. Without the printers nutrients it cannot continue its process therefore being halted.

3.Paper Feeder
The Paper feeder is the feet to the human without it we cannot move forward or adjust ourselves. The paper feeder is what moves and aligns the paper from the tray to the ink cartridges, by having this feeder the printer can ensure that the image is printed on the piece of paper as the User has seen on screen.



Research

By having something like this you are able to improve your crop growth in a more natural way without including chemical compounds to enhance the crops.
