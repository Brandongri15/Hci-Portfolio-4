Unreal
For our assignment, we were tasked to install unreal and follow a tutorial that tasked us all the basics we need to start creating our own project.The tutorial went through the process of an object creation all the way to animating it. Below you will see my progress through the tutorial.





A-Frame
We were given a a premade folder with files inside for us to work on. We were given specific task that got more complex throughout. The task prepped us for VR experiences and shows us how powerful simple web applications can be. Below are results from my task 1-4

Task 1 | Hello World
I increased the length of rectangle to give a springboard effect.

Task 2 | Shape
I just wanted to create a maze of all the possible shapes inside of a-frame

Task 3 | Textures
We learned how to apply texture to the rectangle to make it look like a plank of wood and learned how to apply texture to the plane, with a neat trick to avoid it looking dull and blurry.

Task 4 | Animations
We learned how to rotate the various blocks individually form each other. This was done through property tag inside of a-frame neat framework.

Task 5 | Interaction
Through eventlisteners we can create cool effect that further enhance the interaction. We learned how we can gain inputs in our VR installations.




Task 6 | Interaction Headset
In this task we got learn how we can implement cursor into our headset and have our head be the mouse. We also got the chance to customise our cursor.
